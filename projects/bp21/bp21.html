These animations are part of a weekly newsletter, published for the students of the Fine Arts Dept. in my academy. I looked for a format that would allow some play within the format of e-mail. By using a mosaic of GIFs, every animation is a little bit different, according to the loading times of the page.

Rules:
1: The GIFs have to be compressed to fit a regular e-mail
2: The amount of GIFs has to be the same as the amount of news items
