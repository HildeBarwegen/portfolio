Book, exhibition
Exhibited for one day in Copernicuslaan, Den Haag
Spring 2014
Photographs of the installment by Hilde Barwegen

In the spring of 2014, I moved to The Hague. On my walks in this new neighborhood I came across some very peculiar situations. I exhibited the photographs I made of these new discoveries in the new street, to share my serendipitous discovery of the surroundings.

PS: The '666' photograph was removed within 24 hours. After mounting it for a second time, it was gone within two days, again.
