Installation
Exhibited at the Ministry of Finance and Haagse Kunstkring, Den Haag
March 2016

The labor market is constantly in development: project-based work, robot workers and increasing globalization are just some things that are happening. Wat will be the job of the future human and how will that human approach her or his job? The soft skills advocated by job advisers come across as vacuous terms in a time where it can be extremely difficult to find a job.
