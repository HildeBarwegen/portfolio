Poster
2015

Every year, for thirteen years in a row, Pristina has had a student film and theater festival called “Skena Up”. I learned about this when I was there and met Vullnet, who runs satirical website kukunews.net and organises this event. He asked me to make a poster, which I happily did. This year however the government stopped funding them, which made it much more difficult to continue. As a protest, they would only show interrupted and unfinished films and plays. Unfortunately the event could not yet be organized with their current problems, so the festival really is interrupted. To be continued
