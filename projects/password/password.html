Installation
Exhibited at ‘The Monument’, a one-day exhibition with speculative monuments of KABK students
2016
Voice: Oscar Juul-Sørensen
Poster: Yacinth Pos
Model: Rik Laging

All people lose keys. The same goes up for passwords. While a key is industrially standardized, a password tends to be more personal. This shrine is a place of reflection and reminiscence. The visitor can answer control questions posed by the well in an attempt to recall their forgotton passwords, or to lament the lost ones.
