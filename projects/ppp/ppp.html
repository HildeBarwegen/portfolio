A research in thirds. I dived into an obsession of composition, where everything would be split evenly into parts of three. The book goes through my process, where the rule of thirds gradually becomes clearer in the photographed compositions.

The Gradient Drawing Tool is actually derived from this project, where I intended to make a tool to let others compose with the images provided.
